#1 Spotlight

SOLO LEVELING

Season 2: Arise from the Shadow

Sung Jin-Woo, once the weakest hunter, steadily grows stronger with newfound powers while trying to keep them hidden. Surviving a deadly dungeon draws the attention of guilds, just as a long-lost hunter returns with a warning of impending catastrophe. With danger looming, Jin-Woo must keep leveling up to protect his mother and face what’s coming.

#2 Spotlight

KAIJU NO.8

Season 2

After joining the Defense Force’s First Division, Kafka Hibino, the first kaijuu to side with humans, faces skepticism from his comrades. During a mission against a massive kaijuu horde, he struggles to transform, and the deadly Kaijuu No. 9 appears, aiming to eliminate him and steal his power. Kafka must overcome this ultimate trial with the help of his friends to continue pursuing his dream of fighting alongside his childhood friend, Mina Ashiro.

#3 Spotlight

Dr. STONE

Science Future Part 2

After a tense standoff, a ceasefire allows Senkuu’s team to establish Corn City in North America. Meanwhile, Senkuu and his allies race through the Amazon to build Superalloy City and advance their spaceship project, facing dangerous foes and relying on unexpected allies to uncover the mystery behind the petrification phenomenon.

#4 Spotlight

ONE PIECE

Egghead Arc

After the execution of Gold Roger, the Pirate King, the world learns of the legendary treasure One Piece, sparking the Grand Age of Pirates. Among them is Monkey D. Luffy, a 17-year-old whose goal isn’t plunder but adventure. Following his childhood dream, Luffy and his crew sail the Grand Line, facing fierce enemies and uncovering mysteries, all in pursuit of the ultimate treasure.

#5 Spotlight

DAN DA DAN

Season 2

While exorcising Jin Enjouji’s family home, Momo Ayase narrowly escapes an abduction, and Okarun and Jiji are ambushed by the sinister Kitou family. As they delve deeper into the town’s supernatural mysteries, the trio must rely on their unique abilities to survive and uncover the secrets hidden within the cursed estate.

#6 Spotlight

FRIEREN: Beyond Journey's End

Season 2

Yoichi Isagi misses his chance to take his high school team to nationals after passing instead of shooting. Soon after, he’s invited to Blue Lock, a controversial program where 300 U-18 strikers compete to become Japan’s ultimate forward. Driven by regret and ambition, Isagi must rise to the top—even if it means shattering the dreams of others.

#7 Spotlight

GACHIAKUTA

Rudo, living in the slums of a wealthy town, despises the upper class’s wastefulness and scavenges through their trash to save anything from the deadly Abyss, where discarded items—and people—are dumped. After being falsely accused of murdering his foster father Regto, Rudo is thrown into the Abyss. Surviving its monstrous inhabitants with the help of Enjin, a Cleaner, Rudo takes up the fight to become a Cleaner himself and seek revenge for Regto’s death.

#8 Spotlight

THE SUMMER HIKARU DIED

Six months after his best friend Hikaru vanished and mysteriously returned with no memory, Yoshiki realizes this isn’t the same Hikaru he once knew. When confronted, “Hikaru” reveals a monstrous form but insists he means no harm. Despite his supernatural nature, he shows childlike wonder for human experiences, and Yoshiki clings to him, desperate not to lose him again—yet a dark, otherworldly presence seems to have followed him back from the mountains.
